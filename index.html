Welcome to Wikar

Wikar is an AR app designed for viewing cloud-hosted media such as artworks and design proposals.

If you're seeing this website after scanning a QR code, download the app and rescan to see the content.

Wikar requires an iOS or Android device that supports AR (Augmented Reality). The respective app store links will be able to tell your device's compatibility.
